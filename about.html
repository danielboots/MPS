About

Welcome to Music Production Service, An international firm of Music Producers, Mastering Engineers and Remix Teams specialising in all aspects of Music Production Services including : Full track Production / Remixing / Engineering / Vocal Production / Mastering and PR 

Built on a passion for Music

Music Production Service has been around for some 10 years now and has become a name within the music community as a company who delivers. Owned by Dan Thomas Number one Billboard producer and remixer. Music Production service has become synonumus with artists who need results. Dans has developed a highly successful career in music and is respected world wide as someone who is as knowledgeable infront of his favourite DAW as he is in the professional business side of the industry.

Having forged a successful career in the music industry himself, Dan now employs and looks after acts around the world and is now focusing on bringing his expertise into music technology through his technology brand VUHZE. VUHZE currently is developing software applications for musicians with Dan actively learning new software development languages, watch this space.

Its Dans aim to bring all of this together in order to help the next generation of musicians follow their passion of working in the music business.
